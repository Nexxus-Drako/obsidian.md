The Commonwealth itself proved visually jarring after years of Union aesthetic harmony. Their settlements on Wyrmveld's southern continent Vikatorūa maintained architectural styles recognizable from early 21st-century Earth—asymmetrical structures combining glass panels and concrete forms, traffic systems operating with combustion vehicles rather than clean energy transport, information displays using pixelated screens rather than the holographic interfaces standard throughout the Union. <Luna Darke.> She extended her hand in the archaic gesture I had researched prior to my visit. <Your presence honors our community.>

I returned the handshake, noting the deliberate pressure and eye contact that characterized Commonwealth interpersonal protocols. <Thank you for receiving me, Dr. Sorenson.
