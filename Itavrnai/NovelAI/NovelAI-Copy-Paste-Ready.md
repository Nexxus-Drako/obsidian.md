## ESSENTIAL TECHNICAL NOTES FOR AI GENERATION

ANGLE BRACKET CONVENTIONS:
- . <solo thought> = Luna/character internal thought
- . <transmission> = consciousness-to-consciousness
- <<formal transmission>> = council-level or large-scale official communication

PERIOD BEFORE BRACKET/QUOTE:
- Always: . <thought not just <thought
- Always: . "dialogue" not just "dialogue"

CAPITAL AFTER OPENING:
- Always: . <This is capitalized properly
- Never: . <this starts lowercase

NARRATIVE DISTANCE MARKERS:
- Immediate action = short sentences, present tense focus
- Reflection = longer sentences, broader temporal perspective
- Intimate moments = closer perspective, sensory detail
- Political/strategic = systematic language, options considered
