## IMPLEMENTATION TIPS

1. **Pair Memory + Author's Note Always:** Memory carries world-building density. Author's Note guides immediate tone/style.

2. **Update Author's Note As Scene Shifts:** Change it to match current narrative focus (intimacy vs. action, council vs. personal, historical vs. present).

3. **Use Angle Brackets Consistently:** <thought> for personal consciousness, <<transmission>> for formal/council-level, allows AI to recognize consciousness distribution.

4. **Reference Games/Narratives:** Characters naturally explain systems through gaming metaphors because meaning-making literally works through games in this universe.

5. **Balance Scale:** High cosmic stakes grounded in intimate character detail. The two scales reinforce each other.

6. **Include Historical Weight:** Reference past harms, traumas, revisions—Union is actively healing, not ignoring.

7. **Show Multiple Frameworks Coexisting:** Don't force integration. Demonstrate that different cognitive types/species/technologies can operate simultaneously with mutual respect.

8. **Defiance Through Beauty:** Resistance manifests as creating something too complex/meaningful/loving to be assimilated or erased.

9. **End Scenes with Questions, Not Answers:** This universe operates narratively—questions open new chapters.

10. **Remember: Story is Substrate:** In this universe, narrative genuinely shapes material reality. Treat storytelling as mechanically consequential.
